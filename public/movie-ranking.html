The Best Movie According to Leo

My top 3 movies of all-time

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter movies are about a boy wizard who studies at Hogwarts and fights the dark wizard Voldemort with his friends.

2012

2012 is about a man trying to save his family while global disasters destroy the Earth and people race to escape on survival arks.

Demon Slayer Mugen Train

Mugen Train is about Tanjiro and his friends fighting a demon on a train and losing Rengoku in a battle against a powerful Upper Moon.
